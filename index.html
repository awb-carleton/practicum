Welcome to Practicum!

On this site you can practice problems related to introductory
programming through our in-depth learning environment.

Click on a problem to get started!

Choose the difficulty for the chosen problem

The Guided difficulty will walk you through the entire problem, showing how Practicum works and how it goes about solving the problem. The Independent difficulty will prompt you for input at each step and is a more interactive difficulty. If this is your first time answering this type of problem, we recommend trying it out on Guided difficulty first.
